Romantic Getaways: Start Your Forever

Curated packages for unforgettable beginnings in the most beautiful settings.

Udaipur, Rajasthan - The Lake City Romance

Experience royal luxury, sunset boat rides on Lake Pichola, and the grandeur of the City Palace.

Location: Rajasthan, India
Budget: ₹45,000 - ₹80,000
Transportation: Flight to Udaipur (UDR)
Hotel: The Oberoi Udaivilas
Sites: Lake Pichola, Jag Mandir, Sajjangarh Fort

Andaman Islands - Tropical Seclusion

Pristine white sands, turquoise waters, and world-class diving spots for a private, adventurous escape.

Location: Andaman & Nicobar
Budget: ₹55,000 - ₹95,000
Transportation: Flight to Port Blair, then Private Ferry
Hotel: Taj Exotica Resort & Spa
Sites: Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach, Kala Pathar

Kerala - Backwaters and Tea Trails

Relax on a private houseboat in Alleppey and enjoy the serene, misty tea gardens of Munnar.

Location: South India
Budget: ₹35,000 - ₹60,000
Transportation: Flight to Cochin, then Cab & Houseboat
Hotel: Kumarakom Lake Resort
Sites: Alleppey Backwaters, Eravikulam National Park, Thekkady Spice Plantations

Himachal Pradesh - Snowy Peaks & Cozy Stays

Romantic strolls on the Mall Road and adventure sports against a backdrop of stunning snow-capped mountains.

Location: North India, Himalayas
Budget: ₹30,000 - ₹50,000
Transportation: Train/Flight to Chandigarh/Kullu, then Cab
Hotel: Wildflower Hall, Shimla
Sites: Rohtang Pass, Hadimba Temple, Mall Road

Jaisalmer - Desert Starry Nights

A unique experience with desert safaris, sleeping under the stars, and exploring the magnificent Golden Fort.

Location: Rajasthan, India
Budget: ₹30,000 - ₹45,000
Transportation: Overnight Train or Flight to Jodhpur, then Cab
Hotel: Suryagarh Jaisalmer
Sites: Jaisalmer Fort, Sam Sand Dunes, Gadisar Lake
