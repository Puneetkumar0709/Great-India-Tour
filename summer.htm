Cool Escapes & Sunny Retreats

Beat the heat with our hand-picked summer destinations across mountains and beaches.

Ladakh - The Land of High Passes

Perfect for high-altitude adventure, stunning monasteries, and serene landscapes when the snow melts.

Location: Ladakh, J&K
Budget: ₹35,000 - ₹60,000
Transportation: Flight to Leh, then Jeep/Bike
Sites: Pangong Tso, Nubra Valley, Magnetic Hill

Darjeeling - Queen of the Hills

Enjoy the cool climate, world-famous tea gardens, and the spectacular view of Kanchenjunga.

Location: Darjeeling, West Bengal
Budget: ₹20,000 - ₹35,000
Transportation: Flight to Bagdogra, then Cab/Toy Train
Sites: Tiger Hill, Batasia Loop, Himalayan Zoo

Kerala - God's Own Country

A blend of tranquil backwaters, lush green hills, and beautiful beaches for a relaxing summer break.

Location: Munnar & Alleppey, Kerala
Budget: ₹28,000 - ₹45,000
Transportation: Flight to Cochin, then Cab/Bus
Sites: Munnar Tea Estates, Alleppey Houseboats, Varkala Beach

Sikkim - Himalayan Serenity

A hidden gem offering pristine natural beauty, ancient Buddhist monasteries, and incredible mountain treks.

Location: Gangtok & Lachung, Sikkim
Budget: ₹30,000 - ₹50,000
Transportation: Flight to Bagdogra, then Cab (permit required)
Sites: Tsomgo Lake, Nathu La Pass, Rumtek Monastery

Andaman Islands - Tropical Paradise

Clear turquoise waters, white sand beaches, and excellent opportunities for snorkeling and scuba diving.

Location: Port Blair & Havelock Island
Budget: ₹40,000 - ₹75,000 (Includes Flights)
Transportation: Flight to Port Blair, then Ferry
Sites: Radhanagar Beach, Cellular Jail, Scuba Diving Spots
