Heritage & Culture: Journey Through India's Past

Explore ancient empires, magnificent architecture, and vibrant traditions.

Jaipur, Rajasthan - The Pink City

A vibrant tapestry of royal history, grand palaces, and stunning forts that showcase Rajput architecture.

Location: North India
Budget: ₹25,000 - ₹45,000
Transportation: Flight to Jaipur (JAI) or Train
Sites: Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar

Varanasi, UP - India's Spiritual Heart

One of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities. Experience the ancient rituals on the Ganga river ghats.

Location: North India (Ganga River)
Budget: ₹20,000 - ₹35,000
Transportation: Flight to Varanasi (VNS) or Train
Sites: Dashashwamedh Ghat, Sarnath, Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Hampi, Karnataka - Vijayanagara Glory

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring the stunning ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire amidst a surreal boulder landscape.

Location: Karnataka, South India
Budget: ₹22,000 - ₹38,000
Transportation: Train to Hospet, then local transport
Sites: Virupaksha Temple, Stone Chariot, Elephant Stables

Khajuraho, MP - Medieval Temples

Famous for its exquisite, detailed temple sculptures, representing the height of artistic expression of the Chandela dynasty.

Location: Central India
Budget: ₹20,000 - ₹32,000
Transportation: Flight to Khajuraho (HJR) or Train to Satna
Sites: Western Group of Temples, Kandariya Mahadeva Temple

Kolkata, WB - The City of Joy

A journey through British colonial architecture, deep intellectual heritage, and vibrant local Bengali culture.

Location: East India
Budget: ₹25,000 - ₹40,000
Transportation: Flight to Kolkata (CCU) or Train
Sites: Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, Mother House, Indian Museum
