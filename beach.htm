Sun, Sand, and Sea: Beach Paradise

Discover the finest coastlines and turquoise waters for your perfect tropical escape.

Goa - The Classic Beach Destination

Famous for its vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and varied beaches—from bustling Calangute to serene Palolem.

Location: West Coast of India
Budget: ₹25,000 - ₹50,000
Transportation: Flight to Dabolim/MOPA or Train to Madgaon
Sites: Baga Beach, Old Goa Churches, Dudhsagar Falls

Havelock Island - Tropical Seclusion

Home to Asia's best beaches. Perfect for snorkeling, scuba diving, and relaxing in pristine, quiet surroundings.

Location: Andaman & Nicobar Islands
Budget: ₹40,000 - ₹75,000 (Incl. flights)
Transportation: Flight to Port Blair, then Ferry
Sites: Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach, Scuba Spots

Varkala - The Cliffside Oasis

A unique beach experience where cliffs meet the Arabian Sea. Known for mineral springs, yoga, and relaxed cafes.

Location: Kerala, South India
Budget: ₹20,000 - ₹35,000
Transportation: Flight to Trivandrum, then Train/Cab
Sites: Varkala Cliff, Papanasam Beach, Janardanaswamy Temple

Gokarna - The Tranquil Alternative

A holy town with four pristine, naturally-shaped beaches (Om Beach, Half Moon Beach) ideal for quiet backpacking and trekking.

Location: Karnataka, West Coast
Budget: ₹18,000 - ₹30,000
Transportation: Train to Gokarna Road or Cab from Goa
Sites: Om Beach, Mahabaleshwar Temple, Kudle Beach

Tarkarli - Coastal Maharashtra

Known for its white sands, clear waters, and affordable water sports, including scuba diving and snorkeling near Sindhudurg Fort.

Location: Maharashtra, Konkan Coast
Budget: ₹20,000 - ₹35,000
Transportation: Train to Kudal/Sawantwadi, then Bus/Cab
Sites: Tarkarli Beach, Sindhudurg Fort, Devbagh Sangam
