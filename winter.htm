Winter Adventures in India

Explore snow-capped peaks, frozen lakes, and thrilling winter sports.

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

The ultimate destination for snow lovers and adventure seekers. Enjoy paragliding, skiing, and trekking in the Solang Valley.

Location: Manali, HP
Budget: ₹25,000 - ₹40,000
Transportation: Volvo Bus from Delhi/Cab
Sites: Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass (Closed in heavy snow), Hadimba Temple

Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir

Known as the 'Meadow of Flowers', Gulmarg transforms into a premium skiing and snowboarding paradise in winter.

Location: Gulmarg, J&K
Budget: ₹45,000 - ₹70,000
Transportation: Flight to Srinagar, then Cab/Jeep
Sites: Gulmarg Gondola, Apharwat Peak, Frozen Lake

Auli, Uttarakhand

The gentle slopes of Auli offer stunning views of Nanda Devi and make it ideal for beginners learning to ski.

Location: Auli, Uttarakhand
Budget: ₹30,000 - ₹55,000
Transportation: Train to Haridwar/Dehradun, then Cab/Cable Car
Sites: Auli Artificial Lake, Chattrakund, Joshimath Cable Car
